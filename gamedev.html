Game Development

From player to creator. This is a showcase of my journey into building immersive worlds and interactive experiences.

Player-Centric Design

I believe the best games are born from empathy. My focus is on creating intuitive controls and satisfying feedback loops that make the player feel powerful and connected to the world.

Systems & Mechanics

I'm fascinated by the intricate systems that make games tick. From crafting a balanced inventory system to designing emergent AI behavior, I love building the underlying logic that creates compelling gameplay.

Iterative Development

Great games aren't built in a day. I embrace an iterative process of prototyping, playtesting, and refining to continuously improve and polish the player experience from the first line of code to the final build.
